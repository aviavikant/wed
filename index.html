Welcome to Alinato Events

Top Rated Wedding Planner & Event Planner in NYC

 

Hi!  Alyssa here.  I’m your new best friend on this journey.

As a wedding planner they call me the “Wedding Whisperer” for my intense attention to detail (borderline OCD).  I will keep you on track of your timeline and well within budget.

Vendor relationships?  Oh yes, I’ve got’em and I pass on the good vibes. Have vendors already? No problem, I work with all vendors to make an amazing and effortless experience at the best cost. Learn more about my wedding planning services below.

Learn More »

My goal – make your event THE most beautiful, memorable and seamless experience of you and your guests lives.

Whether you are planning an intimate gathering for a few friends or throwing a 300 person wedding- I will make your event a truly unique and customized experience. I offer services in the Tri state area including NYC, Brooklyn, Long Island, NJ, CT and New York State.
